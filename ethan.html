Ethan is one of the main characters you play as, he is a architect and lives with his wife Grace and his two sons Jason and Shaun.
His story takes place in 2011 where his younger son Shaun gets kidnapped by the Origami Killer,
this causes him and Grace to separate and he moves out also he suffers from random blackouts which might be from the guilt of his son disappearing.
Later in the game Ethan goes and stays in a motel and gets a mail from the Origami Killer, in it are letters with adresses on them to different areas,
that are trails he has to complete to get an adress where Shuan is being held, he has four days to complete all 5 trails.
He bumps into Madison who is also staying at the motel and is trying to get info on victims families but she decides to help him.
