The description of the game

The game is made by Bloober Team and published by Konami, it's a remake of the 2001 Silent Hill 2.

The game is about a man called James Sunderland and he goes to a small town called Silent Hill in the state of Maine.
He is looking for his wife Mary who he got letters from saying she is in Silent Hill waiting for him, but she has been dead for three years.
Along the way he bumps into four other people Maria who resembles his wife, Angela who is looking for her mum,
Laura who is a little girl who knows stuff about James's wife and finally there is Eddie who is a visitor but we don't know why he is visiting.

They are each tied to Silent Hill, James is looking for his wife and the two of them would often go to Silent Hill, so he goes around town trying to find his wife and answers.
Maria is a character who you meet along the way, she looks similar to James's wife but her personality is different to Mary's, she decides to accompany James to help him.
Angela is a character who you first meet not long after you arrive in Silent Hill, she is visitor who is looking for a mum and she warns James about going into Silent Hill but he goes in anyway.
Laura is a character who you meet along the way, she seems to know about Mary and she has met her and the two of them got on.
Eddie is a another character who you meet along the way, he is terrified of getting hurt and is another visitor of Silent Hill but it's unclear why he is here.
