The description of the game

The game is made by United Front Games and was published by Square Enix, it was released for PS3 and Xbox 360 in August 2012.

The game is about you play as Wei Shen who is an uncover police officer and it is set in Hong Kong, he is trying to get in with a local gang called Sun On Yee.
He meets his childhood friend Jackie Ma who introduces Wei to the gang, there he meets the leader Winston Chu who lets Wei joins them.
There is also a rival gang called Jade Gang and their leader is Sam Lin who goes by Dogeyes, he used to be part of the Sun On Yee.
