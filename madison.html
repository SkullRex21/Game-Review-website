Madison is one of the main characters who you play as, she is a journalist living alone she suffers from insomnia and nightmares, the only place she can fall asleep in is local motels, according to her file you can find in the game she has been suffering with insomnia since she was a little girl. During the game you check in to a motel and this is where she first meets Ethan and realizing he is the father of Shaun Mars.
