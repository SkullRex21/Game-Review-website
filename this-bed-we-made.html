The description of the game

The game is made by Lowbirth Games, it was released on November 1st 2023, it is a narrative mystery game.

You play as Sophie Roy who is a maid working at the Clarington Hotel in 1958, after she discovers something it leads her into the depths of mystery.
The two main side characters you can interact with are Beth Lambert who is a receptionist and Andrew Rossi who is a conncierge and you can pick who helps you.

Sophie is a nosy maid working at the Clarington Hotel, she finds herself surrounded by mystery about the guests staying on the 5th floor, she also has a sick mum who she looks after.
Beth is one of the receptionist of the Clarington Hotel, she can be chosen to help Sophie throughout the game, she also likes to hear and tell gossip about some of the hotel staff.
Andrew is one of the concierge of the Clarington Hotel, he can also be chosen to help Sophie throughot the game, he likes to speand his days reading books and tells Sophie what he is reading and offers her some books.
