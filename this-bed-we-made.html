The description of the game

The game is made by Lowbirth Games, it was released on November 1st 2023, it is a narrative mystery game.

You play as Sophie Roy who is a maid working at the Clarington Hotel in 1958, after she discovers something it leads her into the depths of mystery.
The two main side you can interact with are Beth Lambert who is a receptionist and Andrew Rossi who is a conncierge and you can pick who helps you.
