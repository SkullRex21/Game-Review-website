The game is about the Origami Killer who kiddnaps and kills young boys and uses rain water to drown them.
The victims are found dead four days after they go missing, the only clues are an origami figure and an orchid placed on their or near their bodies.
The four main characters are Scott Shelby, Norman Jayden, Ethan Mars and Madison Paige.

They are each tied to the Origami Killer, Ethan is a father and his son Shaun got kiddnapped by the Origami Killer, he try to find his son with the clues the Killer has left for him.
Madison is the least connected to the Killer she is a journalist and is write about the killer and the victim families, she comes across Ethan and starts talking to him trying to get information.
Norman is a FBI agent and as been assigned to the Origami Killer case at the local police station, he is trying to catch the Killer and bring justice to the victim families.
Scott is a private detective who is also trying to find the Origami Killer and he is working with some of the victim families to help them.
