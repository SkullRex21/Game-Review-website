Normanis one of the main characters you play as, he is a FBI agent and he is sent to the police force,
to help with the investigation of the Origami Killer. He has a device called ARI (Added Reality Interface), which are glasses to help him in the crime scenes and look at evidence.
He is sent by the FBI headquarters in Washington D.C to help with the case, he also has a degree in criminal psychology.
His main concern is saving Shaun Mars and trying to find the Origami Killer, he is paired up with Carter Blake who is a police lieutenant,
Blake is very closed-minded and is very sure that Ethan is the Origami Killer this creates tension between him and Norman.
